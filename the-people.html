Carole & Colin Booth

Parents of the bride.
"I'm incredibly lucky to have such amazing parents who continuously drop everything to come to my rescue whether that be travelling hundreds of miles across the country, pulling a lego bucket off my head, driving across 5 counties to find a wedding dress, rescuing us from car breakdowns, spending an entire evening (and the small hours) helping with essays (that I definitely did not leave to the last minute), helping us move house even when sick and of course abandoning days out to come over for impromptu BBQ's - although that probably says more about Simon's BBQ skills." JB

Carole's specialist topics: crafts, tiffin, baileys
Colin's specialist topics: dad jokes, "short walks", the Lake District

Ann & Malcolm Forbes

Parents of the husband person.
“Definitely my parents. All round good eggs, double yokers one might say. Dad is dead so if you see him at the wedding you should probably lay off the booze for a little while. On a slightly more serious note (not that I wasn’t serious about dad being dead) they have supported me throughout the years, always looking out for my best interests so I’ve been extraordinarily lucky to have been birthed by my mother and father.” SF

Ann’s specialist topics: Sewing and stuff, grandchildren, son’s cycling behind a car in pouring rain.
Malcolm (deceased) specialists topics: Beer, ornithology, sleeping in front of the TV, Susan Cramner (maths teacher).
