Accomodation

Where to Stay

For those who do not live local, then first things first, you’ll need to decide where about you’d like to stay. You could either stay in the centre of Plymouth and therefore really close to the HMNB Devonport and the mainline train station and coach station, or you may choose to stay closer to the reception venue in Wembury. Below are just a few options.

Plymouth Centre


  • Travelodge Derrys Cross, PL1 2SW Tel: 08719 846 251
    HMS Drake: 10 minute drive
    Train Station: 6 minute drive or 15 minute walk
    Coach Station: 5 minute drive or 8 minute walk
    Best for: Continuing the party post wedding. Presumably all the matelots will be heading to the Union Rooms. Standard.

  • Jury's Inn, 50 Exeter Street, PL4 0AZ Tel: 01752 631 000
    HMS Drake: 15 minute drive
    Train Station: 10 minute drive or 15 minute walk
    Coach Station: 10 minute drive or 10 minute walk
    Best for: Not being next to all the bars yet still being super close to Dominos.

  • The Grosvenor Plymouth, 7-11 Elliot Street PL1 2PP Tel: 01752 260 411
    HMS Drake: 12 minute drive
    Train Station: 10 minute drive or 20 minute walk
    Coach Station: 6 minute drive or 15 minute walk
    Best for: Well, if it's good enough for Pixie Lott...

    • If you are staying over the night before the wedding and fancy a few drinks and/or a meal out then we suggest you jump in a taxi (or drive if you're not drinking - there's plenty of parking) to Royal William Yard. It's only 10 minutes from the centre of Plymouth, and let's face it, it's much nicer! There you'll find lots of options including: Seco Lounge, River Cottage, Le Bistror Pierre and Wagamamas.

    Wembury


  • Camping at New Barton Farm on the evening of the wedding (£15), although good luck putting up a tent after a day of drinking

  • A variety of B&Bs and holiday cottage rentals can also be found in the local area
