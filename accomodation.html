Where to Stay

For those who do not live local, then first things first, you’ll need to decide where about you’d like to stay. You could either stay in the centre of Plymouth and therefore really close to the HMNB Devonport and the mainline train station and coach station, or you may choose to stay closer to the reception venue in Wembury. Below are just a few options.
