The Reception

New Barton Farm

Location


The reception will be held at the Barn at New Barton Farm in Wembury which is around a 30 minute drive from HMNB Devonport.

Transport


For those driving there is ample parking available in the field, however ladies in heels might want to bring a pair of flats so as not to ruin their shoes between the car and the barn. Same goes for any men planning to wear a fetching pair of heels on the day. For those who will require transport between HMNB Devonport and the barn then please highlight that in your RSVP and we will ensure that transport is provided for you.

Again, if you require transport from the farm back into the centre of Plymouth at the end of the evening you must notify us of this in your RSVP. For those staying in Wembury or those who wish to organise their own transport you must pre-book it.

If you think a stomach full of alcohol and a taxi/mini bus ride is probably not the best idea then if you wish you can camp in the field next to the barn for a grand sum of £15. If you would like to camp then please let us know as soon as possible and we can put you in touch with the farmer to arrange this. Fear not there won’t be any cattle in the camping field!

Important Information


There is no mobile phone reception at the barn and no wifi/internet access, so please ensure your transport is booked at least 48 hours before the day. The fields owned by the farm go up to the cliff, so please keep any drunken antics well away from the edge.

Thank you!
