The Ceremony

Church of St. Nicholas

Location

The ceremony will be conducted at the Church of St. Nicholas, HMS Drake, HMNB Devonport. Entry to the base will be via the main gate (Camels Head Gate). You must arrive between 1300 and 1330hrs

Entry Requirements

As this is a military establishment you need to ensure the details you provide when you RSVP are accurate.

On the day you must show photographic ID which includes your date of birth to be able to get in, i.e. drivers licence or passport. These details have to match with those provided when you RSVP’d. If you forget these on the day then you will not be allowed entry – it is vital you remember your ID.

Transport

You will not be able to bring vehicles onto the base. For RN guests, then as long as you acquire the relevant car passes yourselves then you will be able to park. The centre of Plymouth, where there are numerous hotels and both the train and coach station, is only a short taxi drive away.

Thank you!
