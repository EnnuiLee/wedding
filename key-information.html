Key Information

For the Lazy People

If you don't read anything else then read this

  • You must ensure you have accurately filled out your details on the RSVP.


  • The RSVP must be received by 01/05/2018 at the latest.


  • You must have photographic ID (which includes your date of birth) with you on the day.


  • You must arrive at Drake Main Gate of HMNB Devonport by 13:15 to allow the signing in process to be completed.


  • There is no mobile nor internet reception at the barns.


  • It's a cash only bar.


  • If you're not going to be getting the provided transport back into Plymouth at 00:00 then please ensure your transport is booked at least 48 hours previous.


  • Due to one of our guests having an incredibly severe nut allergy, if you plan on bringing snacks (it's the sort of thing I'd do) please make sure they are anything other than nuts.


  • If you have any problems either the day before the wedding or on the day of the wedding please do not contact the bride or groom. The groomsmen and bridesmaids will be happy to help (probably).

Thank you!

Jade & Simon
