Dress Code

What to Wear

Panic! What to wear?! Well if this is your first rodeo (and by that we mean wedding) then you're in luck. Here's a handy guide.

Non-military Guests


Formal attire, i.e. for the gentlemen suits with ties (top button undone at your peril) and for the ladies, please ensure that your outfit is appropriate for a church service as well as dancing the night away.



RN Guests


1 bravos with swords for the ceremony and meal, retiring to 2 bravos on conclusion of the speeches.



Army


Erm, probably your best uniform for the ceremony and then something else for post speeches. Being honest Tom, we don’t have a clue, so we’ll leave you to figure it out.

Thank you!
